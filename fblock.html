F Block

This is an image of the F Block. It is the official entry point of the college. The F block can be found in the East side of campus. This is one of three blocks in this area. There are three separate floors. The library can be found on the second floor of the building. In the library you can use the computers, study in the quiet area or just rent some books. The building has office space for both for marketing and management. The reception is also on the ground floor of this building.
