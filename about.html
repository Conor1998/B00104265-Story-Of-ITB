About ITB

The college first opened back in 1999. There are approximately 3500 students currently studying at ITB, the numbers are increasing as the campus grows larger. The objective of the Institute of Technology Blanchardstown is to provide a wide range of courses to the North West and Greater Dublin area which specializes in Undergraduate, Postgraduate and Personal Development courses.

The campus provides great facilities along with a high standard third level education. The Institute aims to give students positive future career options and to stand by its core values, especially as it makes its transition into the first Technological University in Ireland.

The University will be developed on three separate campuses. These locations will include Blanchardstown, Tallaght, and Dublin City Center. This is the first time a University in Ireland has been formed from three individual institutes of technology. This is great news for current and future students, as they will now have the opportunity to obtain a degree from the first Technological University in the country.
