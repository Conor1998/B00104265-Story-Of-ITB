Sports Building

This is an image of the Sports Building. The sports building has 2 floors. On the ground floor you will find changing rooms and class rooms. The upper floor has gym and a few lecture halls. It is conveniently located next to the sporting grounds. It can be found on the west side of the campus next to the Linc building. You can walk to the building from the main campus or you can just drive around to the carpark.
