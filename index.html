Introduction

In January 2019, the Technological University of Dublin will be formed as ITB, DIT, and ITT merge together. This will be
the first Technological University in Ireland making it an exciting historical event. This website is to commemorate ITB's Origins in accordance with the theme of 'Change'. It focuses on the history of the campus and surrounding areas.It is targeted towards students, staff, and parents.
