B Block

This is an image of the B Block. This building contains a second smaller canteen, computer labs, and office spaces for smaller businesses. The canteen is located on the ground floor whereas many of the smaller offices are upstairs. This building is one of the newer buildings on campus. It is located on the West side of the campus.
