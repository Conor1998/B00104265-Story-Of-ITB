Horticulture Building

This is an image of the Horticulture building. Only Horticulture students and staff mostly use this building. Our campus contains a state of the art facility. Geodomes, poly-tunnels, and a range of different outdoor areas can all be found here. The building is a little out of the way compared to the other buildings. It's located in the South West of the campus beside the carpark.
