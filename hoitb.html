History of ITB

ITB was established in 1999. The cement was not even dry in parts of the building when it was opened. It was the last institute of technology to be founded in Ireland it is located in the Business and technology park. Graduates have found placement in the business park over the years.

ITB is an institute of technology that specializes in engineering, technology, business ,and digital media. The college is constantly evolving to suit the working enviroment. environment developing courses such as creative digital media and digital marketing. There was only one building on the campus at the start, now there are eight along with a ninth currently under construction. The first courses on campus were business and computer courses. There is a much larger variety of courses today that ranges from horticulture to sport courses.

The first staff were hired in 2001 and the first degrees awarded to students in 2003. A seventy-five million euro investment was needed for ITB. It officially opened as an institute of technology in 2004. A marketing plan was launched back in 2006, this is the year ITB opened it's doors to international students.
